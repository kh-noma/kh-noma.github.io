FILM "WHAT CATS THINK ABOUT"

Produced by Nigina Rashidova and Nomundari Khenzee

At NYU Abu Dhabi, cats aren’t just campus animals — they are personalities, icons, and sometimes even rivals. Our documentary takes you into their world, capturing their daily lives as they navigate their routines. From Luna, who melts into students’ arms, to Minnie, the self-proclaimed diva who thrives in the spotlight, each cat has a unique story to tell. Whether it’s Larry and Minnie’s ongoing battle for campus dominance or the quiet wisdom of the older cat Benjamin, this film reveals the hidden community of cats. We ask a simple yet intriguing question: Have you ever wondered what cats think about?
