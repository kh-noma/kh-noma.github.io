FILM "WHAT CATS THINK ABOUT"

Produced by Nigina Rashidova and Nomundari Khenzee

At NYU Abu Dhabi, cats aren’t just campus animals — they are personalities and icons. Our documentary takes you into their world, into their daily lives. From Luna, who melts into students’ arms, to Minnie, the diva who thrives in the spotlight, each cat has a unique story to tell. Whether it’s Larry and Minnie’s battle for campus dominance or the wisdom of the older cat Benjamin, this film reveals the hidden community of cats. We ask a simple and intriguing question: Have you ever wondered what cats think about?
