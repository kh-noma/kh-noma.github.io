In a world where the sky met the sea, Zephyrus, the Vast Sky, and Thalassa, the Deep Ocean, argued over who was greater. Zephyrus carried the sun and stars, while Thalassa cradled all life. Their rivalry stirred storms, threatening a tiny island.

Bird is flying.

An old capybara, watching calmly, spoke: "You both give life, but together, you bring harmony." Hearing this, Zephyrus softened his winds, and Thalassa stilled her waves. From then on, they danced in balance, and the world found peace. The capybara simply nodded and rested, watching the sky kiss the sea.
